How to Play?

  1. The first player to score 100 wins (Total score).
  2. Everytime you through the dice the value gets added up to the current score until you get "ONE" on the dice.
  3. if you get "ONE" on your dice your chance is over and all you current score is cleared.
  4. Be wise and hold your current score, to save it as you total score.
