How to Play?

  1. Two players take turns rolling the dice.
  2. On your turn, click Roll:
    • The number you roll is added to your Current Score.
    • If you roll a 1, you lose all points in your Current Score and your turn ends immediately.
  3. At any time during your turn, you can click Hold:
    • Your Current Score is added to your Total Score.
    • Your turn ends and the next player starts rolling.
  4. The first player to reach 100 points in Total Score wins the game!
